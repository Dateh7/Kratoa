About KRATOA Consulting

KRATOA Consulting is a women-owned advisory and investment firm focused on infrastructure, transport & logistics, energy, technology and social development. We support clients with research-led strategy, capital solutions and delivery planning that promote economic inclusion, sustainability and long-term value.

Learn More →

Our Values

  • • Partnerships grounded in trust and impact.
  • • Client-centred, evidence-based solutions.
  • • Inclusive and sustainable development.
  • • Authentic, accountable leadership.

Our Approach

We combine data, policy insight and proven tools to design actionable, high-impact strategies that unlock opportunity and drive measurable results.

Women in Leadership

KRATOA is intentionally women-owned and women-led. Our leadership brings together deep technical expertise in finance, infrastructure and governance with lived insight into how systems impact women, communities and everyday mobility.

We believe that centring women’s experiences in the design of transport, infrastructure, digital and social systems leads to safer, more accessible and more productive economies — for everyone.

Our Services

KRATOA Consulting delivers high-impact advisory, funding and investment solutions designed to support sustainable, inclusive and commercially sound growth.

Advisory

Research-led strategy & structuring

Evidence-based advisory across infrastructure, transport, energy, digital and social sectors — from strategy and policy to business cases, feasibility studies and investment preparation.

Capital Raising

Equity, debt & blended finance

Sourcing and structuring fit-for-purpose capital solutions — including equity, debt, mezzanine and blended instruments — with a focus on women-owned businesses, SMEs and high-impact infrastructure projects.

Investing

High-impact assets

Investing in commercially sound, high-impact assets that expand access to transport, energy, digital infrastructure, housing and essential services, while protecting capital and building resilience.

Training, Ecosystem & Thought Leadership

Beyond project work, KRATOA contributes to the broader infrastructure and investment ecosystem through specialist training, conference content design and sector-focused thought leadership.

Expert Training Programmes

  • • Gender lens investing for inclusive infrastructure.
  • • Project financing for public infrastructure.
  • • Public-private partnership (PPP) structures.

Industry Engagement

Developing conference agendas, shaping content and facilitating dialogues that reflect current infrastructure trends and move stakeholders towards concrete, actionable outcomes.

Thought Leadership & Podcast

Articles, commentary and the “Investing in Women-centric Transport” podcast share insights on gender-responsive infrastructure, sustainable financing and inclusive mobility — elevating how infrastructure design and access shape economic opportunity.

Aligned with the SDGs

Our work responds to key Sustainable Development Goals, including SDGs 4 (Quality Education), 5 (Gender Equality), 8 (Decent Work & Economic Growth), 9 (Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure), 10 (Reduced Inequalities) and 11 (Sustainable Cities & Communities).
