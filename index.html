About KRATOA Consulting

KRATOA Consulting is a women-owned advisory and investment firm working across infrastructure, transport & logistics, energy, technology and social development. We bridge strategy, finance and implementation to unlock scalable, sustainable impact for clients across Africa.

Our Approach

We combine data, policy insight and proven tools to design staged, high-impact execution plans that protect capital, widen access and deliver measurable results.

Women in Leadership

KRATOA is intentionally women-owned and women-led. Our leadership brings together deep technical expertise in finance, infrastructure and governance with lived insight into how systems impact women, communities and everyday mobility.

Centring women’s experiences in the design of transport, infrastructure, digital and social systems leads to safer, more accessible and productive economies — for everyone.
