Sectors We Work In

KRATOA Consulting partners with clients across high-impact sectors that are critical to Africa’s long-term growth, connectivity and inclusion.

Our Sector Focus

We focus on sectors where infrastructure, finance and technology intersect, and where inclusive, well-structured investment can meaningfully shift economic and social outcomes. Our work spans public and private initiatives, from early-stage strategy through to capital mobilisation and implementation.

Key Sectors

Our team brings experience across national programmes in transport, energy, digital infrastructure and social facilities, working with governments, DFIs, investors and private operators.

Cross-Cutting Themes

Across all sectors, KRATOA integrates gender, sustainability and governance into project design and execution. We recognise that infrastructure is not neutral — it shapes who participates in the economy, who is safe, and who has access to opportunity.

Our advisory and investment work ensures that financial, social and environmental objectives are aligned, and that capital flows support long-term resilience.

How We Support Clients

  • • Translating national and regional priorities into bankable pipelines.
  • • Structuring projects that attract blended public and private capital.
  • • Embedding gender lens and inclusion outcomes into infrastructure design.
  • • Supporting governance, risk and implementation planning across sectors.
