KRATOA Consulting provides end-to-end solutions across strategy, investment design, capital mobilisation and programme delivery — ensuring projects are bankable, inclusive and sustainable.

Strategy & Advisory

Strategic planning, feasibility, business cases, socio-economic research, and policy alignment.

Transaction Advisory

Investment Structuring

Programme & Project Development
