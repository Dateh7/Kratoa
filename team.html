Nangamso Matebese

Founder & Chief Executive

Nangamso Matebese is an experienced strategist and investment professional operating across infrastructure, transport, energy and development sectors. Her work integrates policy, finance and implementation to unlock inclusive, scalable and commercially sound solutions across Africa.

She has contributed to national infrastructure programmes, financing frameworks, blended finance structures, women-focused investment strategies and large-scale mobility and energy projects shaping long-term economic outcomes.

Her leadership is grounded in sustainability, gender inclusion, governance and commercial viability — supporting governments, DFIs, investors and private-sector partners to execute projects with measurable impact.
