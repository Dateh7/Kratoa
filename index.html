About KRATOA Consulting

KRATOA Consulting is a women-owned and led advisory, investment and strategy firm focused on infrastructure, transport & logistics, energy, technology, and related sectors.

Our work is rooted in economic inclusion, sustainability, and the creation of commercially viable, impactful solutions. We co-create strategies and structures that enable capital protection, growth and long-term value for our clients and partners.

Our Values

  • • Strategic partnerships with key industry players.
  • • Client-centric, research-based solutions.
  • • Inclusive, sustainable economic development.
  • • Authentic, accountable leadership.

Our Approach

We prioritise, apply knowledge and monetise opportunity: using proven tools, data and lessons from success stories to design staged, high-impact execution plans.

Women in Leadership

KRATOA Consulting is proudly women-owned and women-led. Our leadership philosophy is rooted in authenticity, collaboration and strategic clarity. We believe that empowering women in high-impact sectors is essential to shaping inclusive, sustainable economic growth across Africa.

Our founders bring over 40 years of combined experience across finance, infrastructure, governance and development — demonstrating that women are not only participants, but pioneers in shaping Africa’s future.

Our Services

KRATOA Consulting delivers high-impact advisory, funding and investment solutions designed to support sustainable, inclusive and commercially sound growth.

Advisory

Research-Led Strategy
